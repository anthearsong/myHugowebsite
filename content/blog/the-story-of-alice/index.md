---
title: ✅ The story of Alice
date: 2026-03-15
tags: ["poetry", "nature"]
categories: ["poems"]
---
<div style="color:#555555; text-align:center;">

Jim, Tim, Tom and Tashi

all died yesterday. Yet,

Alice is still happy today.

Sunlight dapples through the leaves

and there are always goldfishes in 

his eyes.

</div>
